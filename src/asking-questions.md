# Questions, Feedback, Reporting Issues 

- Most common high-level questions are answered in the [General FAQ](https://www.redox-os.org/faq/).
- More complex high-level or low-level questions are answered in the [Developer FAQ](./developer-faq.md).

If your question is not answered by the General or Developer FAQs please join and ask in the [Redox Chat](./chat.md). It is the best method to **chat with the Redox Team**.

If you would like to report issues, send a message on the Support room of the chat or create an issue [here](https://gitlab.redox-os.org/redox-os/redox/-/issues) and click in the "New Issue" button.
